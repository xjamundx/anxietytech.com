About

Anxiety Tech is a two day conference for technology developers, programmers, health providers, researchers, and students in the fields of mental health and technology who are interested in how virtual reality, artificial intelligence, and mobile applications are changing the way we deal with anxiety, depression, and other mental health challenges.

Anxiety Tech will also address mental health among developers, how technology affects mental health, and what developers need to know to break ground in this area. Join us to help shape the future of mental health care.
